Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about dental care and our services

For most patients, we recommend dental check-ups and professional cleanings every six months. However, this can vary based on your individual oral health needs. Patients with certain conditions like gum disease, a history of cavities, or specific risk factors may benefit from more frequent visits. RAM DENTAL CLINIC will provide personalized recommendations based on your unique situation.

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit cards, and major insurance plans. For extensive treatments, we offer flexible payment plans to make quality dental care accessible. Our administrative team will work with you to maximize your insurance benefits and find a payment solution that fits your budget.

We understand that dental anxiety is common and take several approaches to help patients feel comfortable. These include creating a relaxing environment, clear communication about procedures, gentle techniques, and various comfort measures. For patients with significant anxiety, we offer sedation options ranging from mild nitrous oxide to deeper sedation for complex procedures. Please discuss your concerns with RAM DENTAL CLINIC so we can customize your experience.

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. The titanium implant post that integrates with your jawbone is highly durable and biocompatible. The crown or prosthetic attached to the implant typically lasts 15-20 years before needing replacement due to normal wear and tear. Factors affecting longevity include oral hygiene, regular dental check-ups, lifestyle habits like smoking, and overall health conditions.
