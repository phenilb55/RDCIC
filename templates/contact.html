Dental Emergency?

If you're experiencing severe pain, bleeding, or trauma to your teeth or mouth, please call our emergency line at +91 92650 34801 immediately. We offer same-day emergency appointments for both new and existing patients.

What to Do in a Dental Emergency

  • Toothache: Rinse your mouth with warm water and gently floss to remove any food particles. Take over-the-counter pain medication as directed.
  • Chipped/Broken Tooth: Save any pieces. Rinse your mouth and the broken pieces with warm water. Apply a cold compress to reduce swelling.
  • Knocked-Out Tooth: Hold the tooth by the crown (not the root), rinse it gently, and try to reinsert it. If that's not possible, place it in milk or a tooth preservation product.
  • Lost Filling/Crown: For a lost filling, stick a piece of sugarless gum into the cavity. For a lost crown, try to slip it back over the tooth using dental cement, denture adhesive, or toothpaste.
