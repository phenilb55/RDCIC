Dr. Rahul Machchhar B.D.S

Lead Dentist and Clinic Director

Bachelor of Dental Surgery

Dr. Rahul Machchhar is the founder and lead dentist at RAM DENTAL CLINIC, bringing over 15 years of experience in comprehensive dental care to the Rajkot community. After completing his Bachelor of Dental Surgery, he pursued advanced training in implantology and cosmetic dentistry to provide patients with the highest standard of dental treatments.

Dr. Rahul Machchhar specializes in dental implants, full mouth rehabilitation, and smile makeovers. He is known for his gentle approach, precision, and commitment to creating personalized treatment plans for every patient.

Beyond his clinical expertise, Dr. Rahul Machchhar is passionate about preventive dental education and providing accessible care to underserved communities through regular free dental camps.
